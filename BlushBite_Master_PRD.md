## Bridge Ranking Algorithm
When the Bridge surfaces companions, they are not shown in random or manual order. They are ranked by a score
composed of:
- Fantasy Tag Overlap (40%): Number of the user’s active tags that match the companion’s self-selected
fantasy tags.
- Companion Content Engagement (25%): How many users who consumed similar content then engaged
with this companion’s profile. Collaborative filtering signal.
- Content Freshness (20%): How recently the companion posted content in the relevant category. Fresh
content scores higher.
- Location Match (15%): If user has set or implied a location, nearby companions score higher in physical
session categories.
