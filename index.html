Trusted, vetted dreamers
Every dreamer passes age verification and onboarding before they can reach you. No cold enquiries, no browsers. People who arrive have genuine intent — and they are ready to book.
Fast, transparent payments
Session earnings are released quickly and transparently. No waiting weeks, no opaque fee structures, no deductions you did not agree to. Your rate is your rate — end of conversation.
Complete anonymity, always
Your alias is your only identity on BlushBite. Real name, phone number, and location are never shown to dreamers. Everything is EU-hosted and GDPR-compliant — your privacy is structural, not a promise.
In-person, online, or both
Set your own session modality when you apply. Work from your city, from home, or anywhere with a connection. One platform, total flexibility — change your settings any time from your dashboard.
You set your own rates
You price every session. Duration, type, and fee are yours to define on your own session cards. The platform never negotiates on your behalf, never reduces what you charge, never takes a cut without disclosure.
Build a following with stories & audio
Publish erotica and voice recordings that keep dreamers returning between sessions. Your content builds your brand and your income — companions with active story libraries consistently earn more than session-only profiles.
Gold Verified & Licensed badge
Pass our review and earn the gold ✦ Verified & Licensed badge that appears on your profile. Dreamers trust it on arrival — before they read a single word of your bio. Verification is how trust is built at first glance.
Real analytics — not guesswork
See profile views, story saves, audio plays, and returning dreamers from your companion dashboard. Understand what resonates, when dreamers find you, and what content converts — then grow with data, not gut feeling.

The process
